FAQS

Delivery Details ▼

Delivery is available for most orders pending the number of events we have on that particular day. ​ Delivery and pick-up services for events located more than an hour away will take special approval. ​ There is an additional charge for pick-ups after 9 PM outside 30 minutes of our showroom. ​ The cost of delivery is determined by:

Size of order

  • Labor and Time involved in prepping and packaging the order.
  • Use of our delivery truck, tailor, multiple trips or securing additional Boxtruck/
  • Time and mileage in gas to the event location and back to home base.
  • Unloading items off the truck at the event venue.​​
  • Time and mileage in gas to the event location and back to home base.
  • Carefully packaging and organizing all items back into the truck.
  • Time and mileage in gas back to the warehouse.
  • Unloading all items from the truck back into the warehouse.
  • All necessary moving supplies.

Delivery does not include a styling fee. Adequate access to the delivery site is required. Failure to notify us that more Womanpower is required may result in a fee.

Pick-Up and Drop-Off Policy ▼

Will call (Pick-up) is available for most of our items. ​ The time, date, and location of your pick-up must be approved by The Velvet Willow staff. (We do not have regular warehouse hours.) ​ Rentals must be picked up in a covered vehicle and the client is responsible for proper supplies to ensure the safe transport of our valued antiques. ​ There is an 8% damage protection fee that is applied to your order. ​ Orders over $300 will be charged an additional $50 fee due to labor costs. ​ Orders over $600 will be charged an additional $100 fee due to labor costs to prepare your order. ​ Please take the time to allocate a person to properly wrap breakable items at the end of your event. We understand that sometimes budgets can be tight and that the will-call option may provide you with the wedding or event of your dreams but not at the expense of breaking items that can not be replaced.

Styling and Lounge Cost ▼

Styling Services for the day of the event must be reserved when the rental order is placed.

Drop-off orders will not be styled.

The cost of items online is a la carte price. When ordering more than one item at a time you will incur an additional fee. Lounge Pricing is as follows:

4-5 person Lounge includes: Loveseat, 2 chairs, coffee table, 2 end tables, and Rug: $550

5-6 person lounge includes seating for 5-6 people. rug, coffee table, and 2 end tables: $650

Lux Lounge includes an upgraded sofa and/or screen and/or seating for 8 people, the appropriate number of end tables, coffee table(s), and rug(s): $750

Minimum Order Charge - $250 in peak season. Not applied in Dec-Feb.
