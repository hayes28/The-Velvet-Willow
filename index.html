Welcome

The Velvet Willow is a vintage furniture rental company offering full-service styling, immersive theme ideas, and creative floral designs for any occasion.

Hello

My name is Tiffanie Tippens

I will be your style guide! It is my job to keep things fresh, funky, and fun! I have been styling homes, wardrobes, costumes, and gardens for over 20 years and decided to make art and design my full-time commitment in 2019. In my previous life, I worked as a tech consultant. It took me many years to realize my talent, take a HUGE leap of faith and start living a life of true fulfillment! I am passionate about repurposing elements to give them a fresh look and coming up with out-of-the-box ideas! Life is just a series of moments. I love helping people make the most out of these moments.

What we do

The Velvet Willow is dedicated to creating a dreamy atmosphere for your wedding, event, photoshoot, or set. I aim to transport people into a wonderland by integrating vintage aesthetics with a fresh vision inspired by nature. My furniture is hand-selected so your design will not be duplicated! Whether you are a photographer looking for a creative partnership or a brand looking to win over consumers with jaw-dropping aesthetics, I am here to help. Click the button below to schedule a time to view our studio! I am backed by a team of local artists who are here to inspire funky and fresh new ideas!
